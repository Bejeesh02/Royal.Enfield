Royal Enfield Classic 650

The Royal Enfield Classic 650 offers a single-seat option as a core design element, with a removable subframe allowing for a pillion seat, but often features a sleek solo seat setup with a matching rear fender for that classic look. It's a retro-styled cruiser powered by the familiar 648cc parallel-twin engine, combining vintage aesthetics with modern comforts like LED lights, ABS, and an analog-digital dash, available in several variants with distinct color schemes.
