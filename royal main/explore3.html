Royal Enfield Himalayan 450

The Royal Enfield Himalayan 450 is a popular, purpose-built adventure motorcycle, succeeding the Himalayan 411 with a new, more powerful liquid-cooled engine (Sherpa 450cc), 6-speed gearbox, and modern features like a full-color TFT display with navigation and ride-by-wire, designed for both on-road commutes and serious off-road exploration, available in multiple variants and colors with competitive pricing for adventure seekers.
